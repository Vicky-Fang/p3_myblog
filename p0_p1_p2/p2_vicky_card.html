Giant panda

A bear native to south central China, easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body.

  • Scientific Name: Giant panda
  • Average length: 1.2-1.9 meters
  • Average lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: central China

The giant panda lives in a few mountain ranges in central China which has luxuriant black-and-white fur. Adults measure around 1.2 to 1.9 m long.They typically lives around 20 years in the wild and up to 30 years in captivity.
